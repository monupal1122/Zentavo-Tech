## 4. Landing Page Optimization

For maximum ROAS (Return on Ad Spend), ensure:
- **Speed:** Ad clicks must lead to pages that load in < 1s (Our Next.js setup already does this).
- **Social Proof:** Display testimonials immediately after the hero section.
- **Clear CTA:** Use high-contrast "Get Started" buttons.
- **Relevance:** If the ad is for "E-commerce", the landing page must be `/services/ecommerce-website-development`, NOT the homepage.
